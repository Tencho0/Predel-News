## 1. Executive Summary

PredelNews enters the Southwest Bulgaria news market as a credibility-first, mobile-optimized local news site competing against low-trust incumbents like pirinsko.com and fragmented Facebook news groups. The launch strategy is entirely organic: zero paid advertising budget, a three-person team (founder/editor + 2 writers) producing ~15‚Äì20 articles per week, and distribution through Facebook groups, a Telegram channel, SEO, and short-form video repurposing on TikTok and YouTube Shorts. The core bet is that consistent quality, transparent sourcing, and a clean reading experience will compound into organic search traffic and word-of-mouth sharing within 90 days ‚Äî creating the audience foundation needed to sell direct ad packages to local businesses.

Monetization launches from day one with AdSense as a passive baseline, but the real revenue strategy begins in weeks 4‚Äì6 with direct outreach to 20‚Äì30 local businesses using a "Founding Partner" introductory package (discounted 3-month banner + optional sponsored article). The founder/editor owns early sales. The goal is to reach 5,000 monthly unique visitors and 1‚Äì2 signed direct advertisers within 90 days, proving the model before scaling editorial capacity or investing in paid distribution.
